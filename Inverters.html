SOLPLUS 35 + 50

Efficient, high-performance, operationally safe


The single-phase SOLPLUS 35 + 50 inverters are perfect for small and medium-sized systems and cover the power classes from a nominal AC output of 2.5 kW to 5.5 kW.
They are characterised by integrated interfaces and high failure safety. This is proven by more than 20,000 installations around the world.

SOLPLUS 100 + 120

Efficient, high-performance, robust

Our 3-phase SOLPLUS 100 + 120 inverters are perfect for classic house roofs and industrial plants in solar parks. They cover a performance range between 8 and 14 kW. With its low weight and integrated communication connections, this PV inverter permits quick service even in large facilities. Network management functions - e.g. reduction when the maximum feed in output is exceeded -, reactive power feed in and Ethernet communication connection are integrated by default.

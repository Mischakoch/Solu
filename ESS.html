Smartly storing energy and using it directly

The advantages of our SOLENERGY STORAGE systems:

SOLENERGY STORAGE is a modular and flexible solution to produce, store and directly use solar power. PV hybrid inverters, AC hybrid inverters, battery types and capacity of the accumulators can be individually adjusted to the requirements and the wallet of the user. In addition to the dedicated Solutronic Energy lithium ion accumulators, cost-efficient lead batteries or other battery types can be used as well. In connection to our lithium accumulators of type "SPICA" or "VEGA" with a net storage capacity of 2.5 kWh to 16 kWh, the storage capacity is variably adjusted to your needs

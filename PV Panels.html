Solar Solutions

Economical & Ecological

Produced in our Respected Partner, IFRISol's Tunisian plant, our Polycrystalline PhotoVoltaic Panels are produced to the highest European standards and specifically made to endure

- high temperature location
- high wind pressure (up to 2400 Pa wind load)
- hail and snow resistant (up to 5400Pa snow load)
- and outstanding weather resistance, such as that experienced in Cyprus' hot summers and stormy, cold winters.

Their Positive Power Tolerance is guaranteed to 0 - 5 Wp,
and they boast excellent low light performance

FCP 500

Efficient, high-performance, robust


This technology comes from the well respected SacredSun battery company in Furukawa, Japan. With advanced lead carbon technology, product design and manufacturing experience, they produce a high performance AGM VRLA battery with deep cycle for energy storage system. It has a super long cycle life, using long-life technology and design, with more than 4200 cycles @ 70% DOD, and a design life of 15 years.

Using lead carbon technology, improved charge acceptance ability, and reduced negative plate sulphation, it is more suitable for the partial state of charge (PSOC) application.
Its advanced manufacturing technology and strict manufacturing process, ensure the consistency and reliability of the product.
The modular design and easy installation, aswell as compact structure, saves on installation area and space, time, and convenient maintenance.

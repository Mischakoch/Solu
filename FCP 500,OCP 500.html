FCP 500

Efficient, high-performance, robust


This technology comes from the well respected SacredSun battery company in Furukawa, Japan. With advanced lead carbon technology, product design and manufacturing experience, they produce a high performance AGM VRLA battery with deep cycle for energy storage system. It has a super long cycle life, using long-life technology and design, with more than 4200 cycles @ 70% DOD, and a design life of 15 years.

Using lead carbon technology, improved charge acceptance ability,and reduced negative plate sulphation, it is more suitable for the
partial state of charge (PSOC) application.
Its advanced manufacturing technology and strict manufacturing process, ensure the consistency and reliability of the product.
The modular design and easy installation, aswell as compact structure, saves on installation area and space, time, and convenient maintenance.

OCzP 500

Effective, economical option


OPzV series Gel batteries use German tubular pure gel technology, with the key raw material being imported directly from Europe tothe sacred Sun factory in Japan. Key production equipment isalso imported from Europe. Practice shows that the battery has superior service life and reliability, good cycling performance and charge acceptance ability. It can be used in high-low temperature environment, poor power conditions and various types of communication base stations. It has a than 1600 cycles @ 50% DOD, and a design life of 6 years.

Lithium-Iron-Phosphate Cell
(LiFePO4)

The Elite - proven, durable, resilient

Lithium-iron-phosphate-cells are characterised by a very high resilience and more than 6.000 full charge and discharge cycles. In home-storage systems and when used for commercial areas, this corresponds to a design life time span of 20 years, assuming that the temperatures according to the specifications as well as the charge and discharge currents are not exceeded.
The prism pouch cells are available at different capacities, extremely robust and UN 38.3 certified. They have been used for years, mostly in the Asian and EU area, specifically for electric buses.

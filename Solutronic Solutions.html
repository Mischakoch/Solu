and rapid EV charging

Zero Export

Limit your feed-in with a zero export option

The Solutronic zero export Management solution offers an export limitation option (Feed-in Limitation/Zero Export), integrated in the Solutronic inverter firmware, which dynamically adjusts PV power production. This allows system owners to install PV systems of any size, while ensuring the power exported to the grid does not exceed the limitation OR does not enter the grid at all.
